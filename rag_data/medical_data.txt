Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. 
Symptoms include cough with phlegm, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing.

Medical advice:
Rest well, drink fluids, and seek medical attention if symptoms worsen.

---

Skin Rash is a change in the color or texture of the skin.
It can be caused by allergies, infections, or autoimmune diseases.

Medical advice:
Keep the skin clean, avoid irritants, and consult a dermatologist if the rash persists.

---

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how the body processes blood sugar.
Common symptoms include increased thirst, frequent urination, and fatigue.

Medical advice:
Maintain a healthy diet, monitor blood sugar levels regularly, and exercise daily.

---

Conjunctivitis is inflammation of the conjunctiva in the eye.
Symptoms include redness, itching, and discharge.

Medical advice:
Avoid touching the eyes, wash hands frequently, and use prescribed eye drops.
